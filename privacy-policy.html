Matchcast Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 29, 2026

Matchcast is a sports intelligence and analytics application published by AGDM Soft. This Privacy Policy applies to the Android application with package name com.agdm.matchcast.

Information we process

Matchcast may process the following data depending on how the app is used:

  • App interactions, such as screens opened and feature usage events.
  • App diagnostics and performance data through Google and Firebase services.
  • Device or other identifiers, including advertising identifiers used by Google AdMob and Firebase identifiers.
  • Purchase status and purchase-related information from Google Play Billing for the one-time Matchcast Pro product.
  • Push notification tokens through Firebase Cloud Messaging if notifications are enabled.

Matchcast does not ask users to create an account in the current version and does not intentionally collect name, address, phone number, contacts, photos, messages, precise location, or health data.

Advertising

The free version of Matchcast is ad-supported. Advertising is provided through Google AdMob. Matchcast Pro removes ads. Advertising partners may process device identifiers, ad interactions, diagnostics, approximate location inferred from network signals, and other data according to their own policies and Google advertising requirements.

Analytics

Matchcast uses Firebase Analytics to understand app usage, improve the product, measure conversion to Matchcast Pro, and monitor technical behavior. Analytics events are designed not to include sensitive personal content.

Purchases

Matchcast Pro is sold as a one-time in-app product through Google Play Billing. Payments are processed by Google Play. Matchcast receives purchase status and product entitlement information needed to unlock Pro access.

Notifications

Matchcast may use Firebase Cloud Messaging for app notifications. Users can manage notifications through Android system settings.

Data sharing

Data may be processed by service providers used by Matchcast, including Google Play, Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, and Firebase Cloud Messaging. Matchcast does not sell personal data directly.

Security

Matchcast uses HTTPS endpoints and Google Play services. The Android app disables cleartext network traffic.

Data deletion

If you want to request deletion of data associated with Matchcast, contact support at agdmsoft@gmail.com. Some purchase records are maintained by Google Play and must be managed through the user's Google account and Google Play settings.

Children

Matchcast is not designed for children and is intended for sports analytics users and adults.
